About the Temple

Shri Siddhi Subrahmanyeshwara Devasthanam is a nearly 50-year-old Hindu temple located in Vidyapeeta Layout, Bengaluru. The temple is dedicated to Lord Shri Siddhi Subrahmanyeshwara Swamy and also houses the sacred shrine of Shri Samba Sadashiva. Alongside the presiding deities, the sacred space features beautifully sculpted Prakara Vigrahas crafted by traditional Mysore artisans. For nearly five decades, the temple has been a revered place of worship, devotion, and spiritual service for devotees from Bengaluru and beyond.

Temple Sevas & Poojas

At Shri Siddhi Subrahmanyeshwara Devasthanam, Nitya Abhishekam is offered daily to Sri Siddhi Subrahmanyeshwara Swamy and Sri Samba Sadashiva Swamy, followed by traditional poojas, alongside sacred observances such as Skanda Shashti, Krittika Nakshatra, Sankashtahara Chaturthi, Pradosha Pooja and Sri Satyanarayana Pooja. Every Tuesday, the beloved day of Lord Subrahmanya, is observed as Mangalavara Seva with Subrahmanya Vishesharchane and Maha Abhishekam

Temple Trust

Om Shri Siddhi Subramanya Swamy Devalaya Trust® is the registered charitable trust responsible for the administration, preservation, and development of Shri Siddhi Subrahmanyeshwara Devasthanam. Established to ensure the continued growth of the temple and the preservation of its religious heritage, the Trust oversees the temple's daily administration, maintenance, religious activities, and community welfare initiatives. The Trust is committed to serving devotees, safeguarding the temple's legacy, and promoting the cultural and spiritual values of Sanatana Dharma for future generations.
